# Supplier Intelligence — Navigation & Aviation Aids

## Sabik Offshore

- **Market position:** Near-monopoly supplier for navigation and aviation aids on offshore wind foundations
- **Scope bundling:** Nav/aviation scope is often tied to the WTG supplier's scope, which significantly limits Procon's ability to challenge it commercially
- **Procon strategy:** Procon wants to challenge this monopoly position — commercial pushback is a standing objective
- **Procon responsibility:** Integration only — not design. Equipment must appear correctly on cable lists and GAs; nav/aviation cabinet interfaces must not imply Procon design responsibility
- **Watch for:** Scope creep where Procon is implicitly assigned design responsibility for Sabik equipment
