# Procon Engineering Department Charter & Quality Manual

**Document ID:** 11092223727 | **Version:** 01 | **Standard:** ISO 9001:2015 (Clauses 8.3 & 8.5)

## Mission
"Zero Harm, First-Time-Right" engineering for LV and mechanical systems on offshore wind foundations and substations.

## Scope — Two Operating Streams
- **Stream A — Design & Development (ISO 8.3):** Original design packages
- **Stream B — Technical Support (ISO 8.5):** Review, procurement, verification for external designs

## Organizational Structure
Three sub-departments:
- Mechanical Engineering
- Electrical Engineering
- Project Support

Mapped to Corporate Stage Gates 0–8.

## Process Architecture (3-Level Hierarchy)
- Level 1: This charter
- Level 2: SOPs (for both Stream A and Stream B)
- Level 3: Work Instructions

## Standards
- WTG foundations: IEC 60204-1
- Offshore substations: IEC 61892

## Risk Management
- Design safety risk: ISO 12100 hierarchy
- Project management risk: budget, schedule, scope creep
- Stream B risks: competence/liability, procurement, change management
- All tracked live in Monday.com
