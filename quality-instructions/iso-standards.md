# ISO & IEC Standards Reference — Procon Engineering

## Active Standards

### IEC 60204-1 — Safety of Machinery: Electrical Equipment
- **Applies to:** WTG (Wind Turbine Generator) foundations
- **Scope:** Electrical equipment of machines — safety requirements
- **Key clauses:** Protection against electric shock, EMC, control circuits
- **Stream:** A (Design) and B (Technical Support)

### IEC 61892 — Mobile and Fixed Offshore Units: Electrical Installations
- **Applies to:** Offshore substations
- **Scope:** Electrical installations on mobile and fixed offshore units
- **Parts in use:** (document which parts are relevant as they're identified)
- **Stream:** A (Design) and B (Technical Support)

### ISO 9001:2015 — Quality Management Systems
- **Scope:** Procon's quality management framework
- **Relevant clauses:**
  - **8.3:** Design and Development (Stream A)
  - **8.5:** Production and Service Provision (Stream B)
- **Charter doc:** `knowledge-base/quality-instructions/engineering-charter.md`

### ISO 12100 — Safety of Machinery: Risk Assessment
- **Applies to:** Design safety risk management
- **Hierarchy:** Used for design safety risk assessment in Stream A
